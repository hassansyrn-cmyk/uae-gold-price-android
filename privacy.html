The app may use third-party services including Google AdMob, Google Play services, and external gold price data providers. These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

Gold prices shown in the app are for informational and indicative purposes only.

The app does not provide financial advice, investment advice, trading services, gold buying or selling services, or official government pricing. Users should verify prices with trusted local gold shops, banks, or official sources before making financial decisions.

6. Permissions

The app may request notification permission on supported Android versions to provide price-related notifications or updates.

The app does not request unnecessary sensitive permissions such as contacts, camera, microphone, or precise location.

7. Data Sharing

UAE Gold Price does not sell personal data. Third-party services such as advertising providers may process data as described in their own privacy policies.

8. Children’s Privacy

This app is not specifically designed for children. The app is intended for general users interested in gold price information.
